Inspirational Speaking with a Comic Book Sci-Fi Twist

The Alias AlejAndro Anastasio lives as an artist and is a national motivational and inspirational speaker. Living his life as a professional artist and all around Champion of Life is great cover for his inter-galactic under cover work as The Guardian of Hope.

His one handed life is an inspiration to all he meets. He has turned his limitation into unlimited super human power!

AlejAndro Anastasio currently lives in Boise as an artist, martial artist, performs on stage and is a maker of music. His many accomplishments include a B.A. in Sociology from the University of Washington in Seattle, a Business Owner, Musician, Philanthropist, Nationally Ranked Pool Shark, Vaudeville Performer, Community Leader, Mentor and a bicyclist who has traveled from Seattle to Chicago. He is a member of Toastmasters International, and a speaker for Kituku and Associates.

Despite his irreversible disability, AlejAndro has devoted his life to teaching, challenging and inspiring people to live a life of greatness in spite of any limitations. Born without his left hand, his positive attitude through the years has propelled him to deliver a message of hope to all who meet him. The greatest message of any superhero is the message of hope and inspiration.

With charisma, AlejAndro shares his message through humor, storytelling, music, and performance. Through his presentations, AlejAndro inspires and empowers individuals and groups to move beyond their real or perceived limitations to truly live their dreams.
AlejAndro Shares a TED Talk

But not if we have disabled thinking. When our thinking is not disabled we can tap into the unlimited power of our thinking and change our reality for the better.
