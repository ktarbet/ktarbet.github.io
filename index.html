ktarbet.github.io ================= Karl Tarbet ktarbet at gmail.com Pisces is the primary author of Pisces. Pisces is a desktop application that graphs and analyzes time series data. Pisces is designed to organize, graph, and analyze natural resource data that varies with time: gauge height, river flow, water temperature, etc. Gradually varied open channel flow ( GVF) in circular pipe.
